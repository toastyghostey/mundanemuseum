SUPPORT

Always Around To Chat.

At the Mundane Museum, we live and breathe off of donations given to us by kind benefactors - be it financial, academic, or just plain critique we’re always willing to listen.

Expand Our Collection.

Think you got something neat to show us? Don’t think we’ve covered everything? Have something of your own to show off? Use the field below to shoot us an email and we’ll look into your mundanity.

Correct Our Collection.

Sometimes we don’t always get things right - to err is to human, and we’re dedicated to owning our err’s. We welcome all good-faith academic critique and analysis of our catalog.

Fund Our Collection

The Museum of the Mundane accepts digital donations towards the non-profit cause of keeping everyday moments preserved.
